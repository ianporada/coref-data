## Details

Sub-corpora (original split):
1. Gnome (no split)
1. Pear Stories (no split)
1. RST DTreeBank (train, dev, test)
1. Trains 91 (no split)
1. Trains 93 (no split)
1. VPC (train, test) <- VPC is a subset of RST
